The Location

Step back in time to a nature lovers’ cottage set in a 150 year old walled garden. The Walled Garden Cottage is found on the shores of Lough Derg, close to the village of Terryglass, Co. Tipperary. Visiting the cottage gives you access to miles of wonderful walks, tennis courts and Lough Derg. 

A Linear Spread

Furnishings

The cottage is one room deep, and it has two double bedrooms both en-suite. There is a daybed in a sitting area that can accommodate a fifth guest. With 800 thread count Egyptian cotton sheets, feather and down pillows, and 5 Star Hotel quality mattresses and linens, every night spent sleeping is better than the last!  The kitchen/living room combo is inviting, with a large wood burning stove, gas stove, dishwasher, and more! The large, antique dining table has seen many fun dinner parties over the years. The cottage has satellite television, high speed WiFi, Bose Bluetooth speakers, washer, dryer and all necessary kitchen equipment. 

Relax & Recoup

Garden

Sip your morning tea or coffee on the terrace that opens onto the Walled Garden. It is furnished with a wonderfully comfortable set of dining table and chairs, sofa, and the ever necessary barbecue! The Walled Garden is kept in immaculate condition with ornamental flowers and trees but it is also a working vegetable and fruit garden. The large green house is a sight to behold, with fruits, vegetables, and herbs growing year round! 

Have a drink at the bar!

The Tavern

The Walled Garden Cottage has its own rustic tavern. You can have pre-dinner drinks here if the weather is not cooperating. The wood stove and background music will keep you warm and relaxed even through the worst that the Irish weather can offer.
